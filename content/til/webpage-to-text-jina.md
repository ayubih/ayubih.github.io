description: "How to convert web pages to plain text for easier copying into LLMs."
---

- On your bookmark bar right click and select "Add Page..."
- Name it something like "To Text"
- In the URL field, paste the following JavaScript code:
    ```javascript
    javascript:window.location='https://r.jina.ai/'+window.location.href
    ```
- Save the bookmark.
